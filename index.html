(06) Frequently asked

questions answered.

How much does a website cost in India?

Landing page projects typically start around ₹40,000, while full custom website design and development engagements range from ₹1,50,000 to ₹8,00,000+ depending on scope, number of pages, integrations and motion. We share a fixed quote after a short discovery call.

How long does it take to design and develop a website?

A focused landing page takes about 2–3 weeks. A complete website design and development project usually runs 5–10 weeks across discovery, design, build and launch — depending on content readiness and revision rounds.

Do you offer SEO services and can you improve my Google ranking?

Yes. SEO is built into every site we ship — clean semantic HTML, fast Core Web Vitals, structured data and on-page optimization. We also offer ongoing SEO retainers focused on keyword strategy, content and measurable ranking growth.

What's the difference between a landing page and a full website?

A landing page is a single, focused page built to convert one audience for one goal — a campaign, product or ad. A full website is a multi-page experience covering your whole brand, services and content. We design and develop both.

Do you work with clients outside India?

Absolutely. We're based in Ludhiana and work with founders and brands across India and worldwide, collaborating remotely across time zones with async updates and clear milestones.

Which technologies do you build with?

We build fast, accessible sites with modern HTML, CSS and JavaScript, and scale up to headless CMS, React/Next.js, web apps and custom ERP software when a project needs it. Every build is optimized for speed, SEO and easy editing.
